**Alternative:** Buy separate tools for strategy and training ($$$)

### 2. Executive-First Design
**What:** Built for non-technical decision-makers  
**Why it matters:** Most AI tools assume technical expertise  
**Alternative:** Struggle with ChatGPT or hire consultants

**Design Principles:**
- Zero jargon in the UX
- Structured questions (not blank canvas)
- Business outcomes front-and-center (not technical specs)
- PDF export for board presentations

**Example:**
- ChatGPT: "Figure out what to ask" → 80% give up
- MindMaker: "Answer 12 guided questions" → 70% complete

### 3. Implementation-Ready Outputs
**What:** Lovable-ready prompts, workflows, ROI models  
**Why it matters:** Most strategies sit on shelves gathering dust  
**Alternative:** Pay developers $10K-$50K to interpret consultant reports

**Deliverables:**
- 5-10 actionable Lovable prompts (copy-paste-build)
- Phased rollout plan (30/60/90 days)

- Success metrics per phase (how to measure ROI)

**Example:**
- Consultant: 80-page deck, no next steps → 6 months to launch pilot
